Obstacle-Avoiding Car with 360° Front Bearing Wheel

By: Aditya Kumar Yadav & Abhinav Yadav

1. Introduction

This project showcases an obstacle-avoiding car with two back wheels and a 360-degree rotating front bearing wheel. The car uses an ultrasonic sensor to detect obstacles and changes its direction to avoid collisions.

2. Components and Their Functionality

  1. Arduino UNO - Acts as the brain of the car, processes data, and controls motor movement.
  2. Motor Driver - Controls the speed and direction of the DC motors.
  3. DC Motors - Provide motion to the back wheels and control car movement.
  4. Ultrasonic Sensor - Detects obstacles and sends distance data to the Arduino.
  5. Battery Pack - Powers the entire system and provides voltage to the components.
  6. Chassis - Provides structural support to hold all the components together.
  7. Wires & Cables - Used for electrical connections between components.

3. Working Principle

The ultrasonic sensor emits sound waves, detects obstacles, and the Arduino adjusts the car's movement accordingly.
